SCHOOL INFORMATION


St Mary magdalene Comprehensive School is situated in Kisumu East Sub County, Kamrongo village a half kilometre from Kibos shopping center. the school is owned and sponsored by the Franscican Sisters of St Joseph Asumbi. It is a mixed boarding and day school that embraces holistic nurturing of young boys and girls. Currently the school is offering Compentency Based Curriculum (CBC) and the school is sufficiently equped for the same. There is a well managed means of transport for the day scholars who reside far from school. Our junior secondary learners are employing the services of proffessionally qualified teachers and are performing their duties with utmost passion.

The pre-school and primary sections are similarly equiped with the relevant teaching staff. The school pride itself of the previous 8-4-4 National examination results in which the school posted above average scores in the four years that it has been managed till the present candidates for the said examinations.
