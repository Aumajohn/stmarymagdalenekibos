Welcome to Compassionate Community Network.

Motto:

Creating and building a network for sustainability

Vision:

To enhance the socio-economic status and dignity of community members in the informal settlements and rural areas and ensure sustainability

Values:

Transparency, accountability, Compassion, love and equality

Our Activities

We do this activities regularly

Every week

Mentorship programs/ counseling

  • We offer guidance and counseling to both peers and individuals

  • We offer psychosocial support for the growth of the person

  • We empower young people to acquire basic education through scholarships and bursary

  • We offer various trainings to community members so as to be job creators

    • Weekends

    Agriculture, training and skills empowerment

  • We offer training on agricultural skills, land survey and agritech and empower agricultural innovations

  • We offer training and financial services to lift local farmers

  • We guide local farmers In all legal matters

  • We help in marketing to empower local farmers

    • On a weeekly basis

    Bussiness skills

  • We offer skills in financial management and costing, savings and loans.

  • Business plans, network to guide on grant applications

  • Marketing and all kinds of business mentorship

    • Weekends

    Sports and advocacy

  • We engage young people in sports such as football, netball, indoor games

  • We focus on promoting talents such as drama and music

  • We work to end gender based violence

  • We work to eliminate child abuse
  • We help community members in mediation and reconciliation to enhance peace and justice
