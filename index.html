Why You Should Trust Us? Get To Know About Us!

St. Mary Magdalene Oasis Of Peace Children's Home and School is located in Kamrongo area Next to Make the Difference Primary school , around 200 meters from Kolola Junction.

It is being run by Franciscan Sisters of St Joseph Asumbi. The finances used to run the school are through well wishers and if you wish to support our ongoing programme you can reach to us through the link of donation below
